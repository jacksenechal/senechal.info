Charlotte Maria Carolina Senechal

  • Born: April 27, 1878 in Atwater, Minn.
  • Died: September 24, 1974
  • Married: William Nels Goodlaxon



Charlotte was the third child of Johann Senechal and Louisa Elizabeth Meyer. Charlotte and William had seven children:




  • Vida married Zalmon Gordon
  • Mordecai
  • Vera
  • Velma
  • Verona
  • Vernon
  • Vergil
