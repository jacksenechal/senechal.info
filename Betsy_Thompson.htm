Betsy Elliott Thompson Senechal

  • Born: March 6, 1920 in Pacific Grove, CA
  • Married: James F. Senechal on May 6, 1942 in Seattle, WA

Betsy Elliott Thompson was the third child of William Francis Thompson and Julia Bell Shands. She moved with her parents from California to Seattle, WA. When WWII began, she married Jim before he went to fight in Germany. When he came back, they lived in Montana while he attended law school. With a return to the US Army, they came to live many places, with major assignments in Germany, North Carolina, and Virginia. Her first career was Army wife, second career was school secretary in Arlington, VA, from which she retired ca 1986.

She and Jim have three children:

  • Virginia Ann Senechal
  • Peter Kerr Senechal married Diana Daffin
  • John Shands Senechal married Karen Hampton, then Anne Terrell
