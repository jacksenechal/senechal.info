Virginia (Ginny) Ann Senechal

  • Born: March 15, 1948 in Missoula, Montana

Virginia (Ginny) Ann Senechal is the first born child of James Frederick and Betsy (Thompson) Senechal.
She grew up travelling with the family, living in Germany, North Carolina, Seattle, Virginia. Ginny settled in Seattle, after graduating from Duke Univ. She works as a freelance editor.
