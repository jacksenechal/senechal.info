Peter (Pete) Kerr Senechal

  • Born: October 18, 1949
  • Married: Dianna Lynne Daffin, June 7, 1971 in Durham, NC

Pete Senechal is the second born child of James Frederick and Betsy (Thompson) Senechal.
He was born on October 18, 1949 in Seattle, Wa. He travelled with the family to live in North Carolina, Germany, Virginia, and Seattle. He graduated from Duke Univ. and Medical College of Virginia. He married Diana Lynn Daffin on June 7, 1971 in Durham, NC. They have two children. Pete retired from the US Air Force in 1996, after a full career, at the rank of Colonel.
