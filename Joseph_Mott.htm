Joseph Brewster Mott

  • Born: 1821 or 22 in New York
  • Died: 1907
  • Married: Irene Nowlin in 1846

Joseph Brewster Mott was the first of two sons of Rev. Daniel Mott and Hulda Worth.
He married Irene Nowlin and had 8 children.

Children with Irene Nowlin:
  • James Mott
  • Edward Mott married Nellie Taylor, then Hattie Walston
  • Daniel Mott
  • Mary E. Mott married William M. Maxwell
  • Simon Mott
  • Hannah Mott
  • Emma Mott married Mr. Buchan
  • Alford Mott
