Amelia (Emily) Theresa Henrietta Senechal Kind

  • Born: September 29, 1876 in Atwater, MN
  • Died: 1956 in West Great Falls, Montana
  • Married: Albert Henry Kind on May 28, 1896 in Fairfield, MN


Emily was the second child of Johann Senechal and Louisa Elizabeth Meyer. She married Albert Kind (b. 7/19/1872, d. 11/21/1946), a carpenter who built the family home in Drake, ND in 1901.




Children:

  • Erwin William Herman Kind married Vina Mae Thompson
  • Adella Louise Kind married Floyd Armentrout
  • Albert August Anton Kind married Norma Henriette Erickson
  • Daniel Edward William Kind married Margaret Amelia Erickson
  • Harold Albert Otto Kind married Rhoda Crippen
  • Luverne L. Kind married Andrew Mainland
