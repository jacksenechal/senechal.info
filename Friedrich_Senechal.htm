Friedrich William Daniel (Will) Senechal

  • Born: July 27, 1875 in Atwater, MN
  • Died: January 10, 1958
  • Married: Carolina Strege



Friedrich William Daniel Senechal, labelled in a 1950 family portrait page as "Will", the first child of Johann and Elizabeth (Meyer) Senechal. Will and Carolina had five children:




  • Harold
  • Howard
  • Waldo
  • Viola
  • William
