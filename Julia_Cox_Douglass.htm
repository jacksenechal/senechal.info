Julia Cox Douglass

  • Born: 1856
  • Died: 1936
  • Married: Ninus Eugene Shands in 1875 in Kaufman Co., TX

Julia Cox Douglass was the youngest daughter of Harry Clinton Douglass and Elizabeth (Betsy) Wynne Elliott.

She and NE had 8 children:

  • Bessie Melissa Shands, died at one year
  • Jonnie Vonda Shands
  • Percy Clinton Shands, married Sarah Leola David
  • Ned Douglass Shands, married Olive Lillian Denman
  • Julia Bell Shands, married William Frances Thompson
  • Ninus Eugene Shands, married Elsie Holley
  • Hilliard Judge Shands, married Annie Marie Townsend
  • Henry Gilchrist Shands, married Janie Hopson
