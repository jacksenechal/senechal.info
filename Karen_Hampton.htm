Karen Lee Hampton Senechal

Karen Lee Hampton Senechal, second child of James (Buck) Hampton and Mildred Jones.
She was born on January 20, 1949 in Gastonia, NC. She married John Senechal in 1978, in Burnsville, NC. Trained as a nurse, she is licensed now as a Family Nurse Practitioner (FNP) and as a Nurse Midwife. She has three children, one brother and one sister.
