John Daniel Anton Senechal

  • Born: October 15, 1879 in Atwater, Minn.
  • Died: October 15, 1966
  • Married: Marie J. Pieske



John Daniel was the fourth child of Johann Senechal and Louisa Elizabeth Meyer. Marie Pieske was the sister of Adele Pieske who married John's brother Robert. Marie and John had six children:



  • Gertrude married Milford Rogelstad
  • Alvin Herman married Ester Winterfeld
  • Walter married Thelma Spielman
  • Irene married William Renz
  • Lillian married Frederick Plankey, then Edward Carl Lauf
  • Ella married George Washington Fors
