John Shands Senechal

  • Born: June 21, 1951 in Munich, Germany
  • Married: Karen Lee Hampton in 1978 in Burnsville, NC
  • Married: Margaret Anne Terrell in 1990 in Asheville, NC

    John Senechal was born on June 21, 1951 in Munich, Germany. His parents were James (Jim) Frederick Senechal and Betsy Elliott (Thompson) Senechal.

    His parents were American citizens in Germany with the US Army occupation forces after WWII. He lived in Germany for two years, moving then to Fort Bragg, NC. Military assignments being what they are, he moved to Seattle, WA at six, then back to Fort Bragg. At twelve he travelled back to Germany for a three year stay, thence to Arlington, VA for three years. He spent one year in college at Duke U. in Durham, then back to Washington DC area for several years, with an attempt at homesteading in WV in that time period. A move to Burnsville, NC in 1978 makes him a returned "Tarheel". He married Karen Lee Hampton and had 3 children there. In 1985 the family moved to Weaverville, NC near Asheville, where he still resides. He divorced and remarried in 1990 to M. Anne Terrell. He makes a living as a contractor.

    He had 3 children with Karen Lee Hampton:
    • John (Jack) Shands Senechal II
    • Katie Rose Senechal
    • Will Hampton Senechal
