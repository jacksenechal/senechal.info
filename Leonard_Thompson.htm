Leonard Ramsay Thompson

  • Born: October 18, 1892 in St. Cloud, MN
  • Died: August 25, 1970 in San Pedro, CA
  • Married: Ruth Rogers Miller in 1926 in Los Angeles, CA

Leonard was one of six children of Pirrie and Janet Thompson. He spent much of his childhood in Everett, WA and went to college at the University of Washington where he received his undergraduate degree in bacteriology. Then in 1921 he earned a doctor of medicine from Stanford University Medical School. During his internship he was House Officer in Obstetrics and Gynecology at San Francisco Hospital. He had post-graduate education in obstetrics and gynecology at the University of Pennsylvania Medical School. He practiced medicine in San Pedro, CA where he was an active civic and medical leader.

His children with Ruth:
  • Arthur Farrand Thompson married Roberta McGregor, then Diane L. Krasovic
  • Clara Gertrude Thompson married Allen Gresham
  • Lois Anna Thompson married Charles Smith II.
