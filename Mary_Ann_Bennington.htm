Mary Ann Bennington

Mary Ann (Martha) Bennington, daughter of Robert Smith Bennington and Mary Elizabeth Haines.
She was the wife of John Yeargy Walston, and mother of Hattie Lorena Walston
Mary Ann was born November 19, 1843; died February 13, 1905.
She lived through the Civil War; her husband was a soldier. She would have been 22, and he would have been 21 when the war ended.

Childrens' names:

  • Robert Daniel Walston
  • William Henry Walston
