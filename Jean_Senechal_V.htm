Jean Frederic Senechal

  • Born: November 26, 1823 in Ploewen, Germany
  • Died: April 26, 1908 in Atwater, MN
  • Married: Henrietta Bettac on May 20, 1849 in Ploewen, Germany

Jean and Henrietta lived in Prussia at the time of King Wilhelm I and Otto Von Bismarck. The country was gearing up for war. Their eldest son Johann served in compulsory military training for three years. At the close of the Civil War in America, the couple gathered their family and moved, in 1866, to Minnesota, USA. They settled in Atwater, MN.



They had eight children:
  • Johann Friedrick Senechal married Louise Elizabeth Meyer
  • Minnie Senechal married Fredrich Hasenbank
  • Lizzie Senechal married Ferdinand Ludwig Menard
  • Caroline Senechal married Heinrich Goldhahn
  • Daniel Friederich Senechal married Ann Muhly
  • Friedericke Senechal married William J. Meyer
  • Anna Senechal
  • Charlotte Senechal married Johann Feig
