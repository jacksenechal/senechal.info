Henry Aaron Terrell


  • Born: March 27, 1888 in Gadsden, AL
  • Died: May 21, 1928 in Kentucky
  • Married: August 25, 1910 to Annie Bell Moor


Henry worked for the railroad, but died young from pnuemonia while working on a new house for the family in Kentucky.


He had five children with Annie Bell Moor:

  • Eloise Terrell married Jack Brooks
  • Howell Henry Terrell married Bernice Elmore, then Olivia Sells, then Joyce Carruthers
  • Loura Terrell married Paul Popham
  • Andrew Pierce Terrell, died young
  • William Jack Terrell, died young
