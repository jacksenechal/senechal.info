Louisa Elizabeth Meyer

  • Born: July 19, 1855 in Niagara, NY
  • Died: June 24, 1948 in Sabin, MN
  • Married: Johann Friedrick Senechal on October 25, 1874 in Atwater, MN

Louisa Elizabeth Meyer was born in the USA of immigrant parents Frederick Meyer and Fredericke Mueller.
The Meyer family moved to Atwater, MN when Johann was there. Elizabeth and Johann married, and farmed in Atwater until 1890. They filed on a homestead in Todd County, MN which they occupied and improved for seven years. Then they sold that property and bought a farm in Swift County, MN. About five years later, in 1902, they went to North Dakota and purchased 320 acres in McHenry County, ten miles north of Drake, ND. where they stayed.
