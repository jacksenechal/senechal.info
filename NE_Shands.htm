Ninus Eugene Shands

Ninus Eugene (NE) Shands was a son to Hilliard Judge Shands and Melissa Boydston Penry.
He was born on November 23, 1848; died on April 10, 1926.
NE came of military age just as the Civil War ended, thus escaping the fighting. After travelling to Texas with his family at the close of the war, he studied medicine at the Louisville, Kentucky Medical School. In 1875, he married Julia Cox Douglass in Kaufman, Co. Tx. They lived in Forney, Texas, where NE practiced medicine.


NE and Julia Cox had eight children:
  • Jonnie Vanda Shands
  • Clinton Percival Shands
  • Bessie Melissa Shands, died at 1 year old
  • Ned Douglass Shands
  • Julia Bell Shands married William Francis Thompson
  • Ninus Eugene Shands II
  • Hilliard Judge Shands
  • Henry Gilchrist Shands
