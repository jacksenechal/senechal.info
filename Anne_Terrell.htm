Margaret Anne Terrell Senechal


  • Born: November 19, 1949
  • Married: November 3, 1990 to John Senechal

Anne Terrell is the second child of Henry and Bernice Terrell. She moved with her parents to Nashville, then to Johnson City, where her father still resides, then to Asheville. She has one brother and two sisters. She is a nutritionist and therapist.
