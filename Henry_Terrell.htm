Howell Henry Terrell


  • Born: May 27, 1923 in Cartersville, GA
  • Died: March 17, 2000 in Gray, TN
  • Married: May 24, 1947 to Bernice Elmore
  • Married: March 3, 1990 to Olivia Sells
  • Married:October 27, 1997 to Joyce Carruthers


Henry Terrell was a son of Henry Aaron Terrell and Annie Bell Moor. He was born in Cartersville, GA. The family moved to Kentucky, where the family lived until the untimely death of his father when Henry was five. The family moved then back to Cartersville. Henry joined the merchant marine corps in WWII, serving in the Pacific. His subsequent career was in oil, working for Sinclair Oil, then Atlantic Richfield. He ran a dealership, Terrell Oil, in Johnson City TN, retiring at 55.


He had four children with Bernice Elmore:

  • Douglas Terrell married Lois Snow, then Joan Schmidt
  • Margaret Anne Terrell married John Senechal
  • Alice Joan Terrell married Kevin Bowling, then Neil Collins
  • Mary Candace Terrell married Howard "Butch" Smith
