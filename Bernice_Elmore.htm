Bernice Elmore


  • Born: February 9, 1923 in Gordo, AL
  • Died: June 15, 1989 in Gray, TN
  • Married: May 24, 1947 to Henry Terrell


Bernice Elmore was one of four children born to William Temple Elmore and Mary Arzula Parker. She was born in Gordo AL, where the rest of the family still resides. She trained as a nurse (RN), but chose to be a homemaker after having children. The family moved several times, to Lakeland FL, to Chattanooga TN, to Nashville TN, to Johnson City TN. She was a gardener, avid reader, and volunteer with the Red Cross.




She had four children with Henry Terrell:

  • Douglas Terrell married Lois Snow, then Joan Schmidt
  • Margaret Anne Terrell married John Senechal
  • Alice Joan Terrell married Kevin Bowling, then Neil Collins
  • Mary Candace Terrell married Howard "Butch" Smith
