Annie Bell Moor


  • Born: January 24, 1890 in Gordo, AL
  • Died: October 22, 1957 in Cartersville, GA
  • Married: August 25, 1910 to Henry Aaron Terrell







She had five children with Henry Aaron Terrell:



  • Howell Henry Terrell married Bernice Elmore, then Olivia Sells, then Joyce Carruthers
  • Loura Terrell married Paul Popham
  • Eloise Terrell married Jack Brooks
  • Andrew Terrell, died young
  • William Terrell, died young
